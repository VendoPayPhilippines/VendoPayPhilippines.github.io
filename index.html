VendoPay Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 8, 2024

1. Introduction

VendoPay is committed to protecting the privacy of its users while ensuring a secure and efficient digital transaction platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app. By using VendoPay, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection and Usage

2.1 Personal Information

To access certain features, users must log in to their account. Registration is available only by contacting VendoPay support directly. During registration, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address

This information is used solely to create and manage your account on VendoPay.

2.2 Transaction Data

When using VendoPay, transaction data is collected to provide transaction history and enable specific digital transactions. Available transactions include:

  • Mobile Load (Globe, Smart, Sun, TM, etc.)
  • Digital Wallet Top-Ups (GCash, Maya, Coins.ph, etc.)
  • Gaming Pins (Roblox, Steam, Playpark, etc.)
  • Bills Payments (PLDT, Globe, Meralco, SSS, etc.)

Each transaction includes details such as transaction type, date, and amount, stored securely within your account history and accessible for viewing within the app.

2.3 User Activity Data

To improve user experience and maintain service functionality, we may collect data on app usage, including transaction history and usage patterns.

3. Account Management

Users may log in and manage their accounts within the app. We also offer a Delete Account option, allowing users to permanently delete their data upon request. For more information, please contact our support team.

4. Security of Your Data

VendoPay prioritizes data security, implementing standard security protocols to protect your information. However, please be aware that data transmission over the internet cannot be guaranteed to be entirely secure, and VendoPay cannot ensure absolute security.

5. Wi-Fi Voucher Creation

Please note that Wi-Fi voucher creation remains unavailable in this version. No data related to Wi-Fi connections or network usage is collected or processed.

6. User Responsibilities and Limitations

While we strive to protect your information and provide accurate services, users are responsible for:

  • Ensuring that the personal data provided is accurate and up-to-date.
  • Maintaining the confidentiality of login credentials to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Using VendoPay responsibly, solely for its intended purpose of digital transactions.

VendoPay reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts found to be misusing the app or violating its terms.

7. Limitation of Liability

VendoPay is not liable for any direct or indirect damages arising from the use or inability to use our services, including issues related to connectivity, transaction processing, or loss of data. Users acknowledge that VendoPay is provided "as-is" during this stage, and while we strive to ensure smooth functionality, issues may arise that are outside our control.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

As VendoPay continues to evolve, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features, changes in practices, or regulatory requirements. We will notify users of any significant changes via in-app notifications or email.
