VendoPay Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2024

1. No Collection of Personal Data

VendoPay is currently in its prototype stage. We do not collect or process any personal data. All displayed information within the app is hard-coded and is not linked to any backend services.

2. Purpose of the App

VendoPay is designed for merchants to sell digital products such as e-wallet top-ups, prepaid load, bills payment, and bank deposits. However, this version is non-functional and serves only as a demonstration of the app's interface.

3. Non-Functionality Disclaimer

The VendoPay prototype does not perform any real transactions, connect to backend servers, or process personal data.

4. Future Updates

As the app transitions from prototype to full functionality, we will update this Privacy Policy to reflect any changes in data collection, use, and storage. These updates will ensure compliance with all applicable privacy laws.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. You will be notified of any significant changes via the app or our website.
